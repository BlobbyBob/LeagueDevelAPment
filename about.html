Data

As data source 59,650 normal and ranked matches from the regions EUW, NA and KR (patch 5.11) have been compared to 59,588 normal and ranked matches from EUW, NA and KR (patch 5.14).

Simplify

To ensure the data were more consistent the following simplification were executed:

  • All boot enchantments got removed
  • All runeglaive enchantments were moved to Ranger's Trailblazer
  • Archangel's staff was upgraded to Seraph's Embrace
  • Banner of Command and Locket of the iron Solari were downgraded to Aegis of the Legion
  • Quicksilver Sash was upgraded to Mercurial Scimitar

Analysis

For the analysis the following categories were declared as important:

  • Winrate and Popularity of Mages
  • Winrate and Popularity of AP items on Mages
  • Winrate (on Mages) and Popularity of boots
  • Winrate and Popularity of AP items, MR items and boots on Mages
  • Damage per Second done with AP items
Mages are all of the Champions listed on the main page if they built at least one item listed under AP items on the main page.

Files

All files used for analyzing can be found in the following GitHub repository: https://github.com/BlobbyBob/LeagueDevelAPment
